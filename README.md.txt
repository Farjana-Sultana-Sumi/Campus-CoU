Campus CoU was designed to provide university informations through an application. It will help them to get any informations quickly and easily through mobile app.

Campus CoU consists:
    1. About CoU
    2. Administration (Chancellor, Vice CHancellor, Syndicate, Registrar, Student Advisor, Proctor Office)
    3. Faculty (Science, Arts and Humanities, Social Science, Engineering, Business Studies, Law)
    4. Facilities (Cafeteria, Mosque, Residential Hall, Others)
    5. Bus Schedule (Bus time, Route)
    6. Send Mail
    7. Contact
    
Software Requirement:
 =>Android Studio, SQLite, xml, Java.
